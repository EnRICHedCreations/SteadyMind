Clinical-Grade Crisis Detection

Advanced bipolar crisis detection system designed with medical accuracy and DSM-5 compliance.

📈 Mania Detection

  • • Critical sleep disruption (≤3 hours while feeling rested)
  • • Extreme mood/energy elevation patterns
  • • Multiple severe behavioral symptoms
  • • Racing thoughts and grandiosity tracking

📉 Depression Detection

  • • Extreme low mood or energy levels (≤1/10)
  • • Hopelessness and worthlessness monitoring
  • • Immediate suicidal ideation detection
  • • Social withdrawal and concentration tracking

How It Works

Our system analyzes sleep patterns, mood elevation, and behavioral changes using a sophisticated multi-factor scoring algorithm. Sleep disruption gets the highest priority as it's the most reliable early indicator of manic episodes.
